Understanding Disc Problems: The L4-S1 Protocol

کمر کے نچلے حصے (L4-L5) میں درد کی وجوہات اور ان کا بغیر آپریشن جدید طریقہ علاج۔

Disc herniation in the lumbar spine, specifically at the L4-L5 and L5-S1 levels, is one of the most common causes of chronic back pain and sciatica. At Spine Care, our approach moves beyond simple exercise. We utilize the Spinal Matrix Reset protocol.

This protocol involves high-precision mechanical decompression to reduce the pressure on the nerve roots. By combining manual adjustments with specialized clinical instrumentation, we achieve a 99.2% success rate in non-surgical recovery.

Neuroplasticity & Stroke Recovery: Reclaiming Motion

فالج کے بعد مریض کو دوبارہ چلنے پھرنے کے قابل بنانا اور اعصابی نظام کی مکمل بحالی۔

Stroke (CVA) recovery is a race against time. The brain's ability to rewire itself—known as neuroplasticity—is at its peak during the first few months post-trauma. Our Neuro Logic Sync therapy focuses on intensive motor-unit recruitment.

We use specialized sequencing exercises to trigger dormant neural pathways. This helps patients regain control over spastic limbs and improves overall gait stability.

Dry Needling: The Science of Trigger Point Release

سوئیوں کے ذریعے پٹھوں کے کھچاؤ اور درد کا فوری علاج۔

Dry needling is a clinical technique used to treat dysfunction in skeletal muscle, fascia, and connective tissue. Unlike acupuncture, which is based on traditional Chinese medicine, dry needling is strictly based on Western anatomical and neurophysiological principles.

By inserting a fine needle into a "trigger point," we can cause a localized twitch response, which effectively resets the muscle and relieves chronic tension.
